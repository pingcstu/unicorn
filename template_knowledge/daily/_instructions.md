# Daily Notes

This folder is for your daily thoughts, reflections, and quick notes.

## Purpose:
- Capture ideas, observations, and tasks throughout the day.
- Journaling and personal reflection.
- Quick logging of activities or important events.

## Suggested Content:
- Dated markdown files for each day (e.g., `2024-02-07.md`).
- Bullet points for tasks, accomplishments, and thoughts.
- Links to relevant articles or resources encountered.
